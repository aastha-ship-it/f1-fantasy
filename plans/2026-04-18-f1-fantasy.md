## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version isn't "more features." It's **"the friend group tells three other friend groups about it."** The product's emotional peak is the REVEAL moment — everyone's locked-in picks flip over simultaneously, like poker showdown, with the race result dropping at the same time. Everything else (leaderboard, standings, telemetry) serves that peak.

### Platonic Ideal
Dark, cinematic, opinionated. Not a stat dashboard, not a copy of F1.com. Its own thing. Typography-forward, motion-intentional, silent where most apps stuff data. The app doesn't try to be everything — it tries to be the group's season-long argument engine. The product's brand is "the reveal."

Persistent archive. Every season's rivalries, called shots, and roasts accumulate. Year 2, when someone says "remember when Vineet picked Sainz for Monza P1 and he DNF'd on lap 1?" — the app has that receipt.

## Accepted Scope (added to the plan)
- **Shareable post-race leaderboard card** — Next.js OG-image route at `/api/share/[eventId]/card.png`. Renders current leaderboard as 1200×630 PNG with F1 theme. Friends can screenshot or share directly.
- **Streak tracker** — `scores_rollup` materialized view or aggregate query: current P1-correct streak per user, longest-ever streak, current points streak. Display on leaderboard and profile.
- **Reveal animation** — Dedicated `/reveal/[eventId]` screen. Cards for each friend's pick, face-down. On load (or on countdown), flip sequentially with ~150ms stagger using Framer Motion. Result cards flip in first, then friend picks reveal. ~30 LOC.
